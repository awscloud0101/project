Green Building

We are committed to instilling the concept of sustainability in our students and staff. Practical understanding by exploration of the concept of solar cells and storage of energy via hands-on experience in fully-equipped labs and also taking up of projects on recycling and waste management.

Botanical garden

Our School proposes to have a botanical garden where possible, which provides children with the opportunity to experience the magnificence and vast variety in trees, plants and herbs. Children undertake the care of plants and learn about soil, flora and fauna and our dependence on them.

Solar Energy System

Citizens of the future should learn about upcoming technologies at an early age. And DWPS Narapally plans to innovate systems which not only generate clean solar electricity but make a lasting impression on the students. Thus every student at DWPS will get a first-hand experience and learn the many benefits of renewable energy.

Rainwater Harvesting System

DWPSNarapally will develop a rainwater harvesting system. Imparting practical education on the importance & need to conserve the Earth's resources. Not just preaching, but paving the way to a bright future.
