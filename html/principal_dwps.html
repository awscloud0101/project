DWPS Narapally Principal's Message

“The future of the world is in my classroom today.” –Ivan Fitzwater

In today’s scenario, more than ever, the role of a teacher and that of a school is one of paramount importance in a child’s life. The 21st century has made quantum leaps in education and information. Today’s world is dynamic and demanding , and to keep pace with it, one has to be adaptive, adventurous and empathetic.

We have to teach our children the skills they need to copy with the future. The 4C’S Communication –sharing thoughts and ideas, Collaboration-working together harmoniously and productively, Creativity- using innovative methods to tackle situations and Critical Thinking-examining problems and finding solutions in different ways- are the need of the hour.

This is the education that we at DWPS, Narapally ,seek to impart to our children. We try to combine the latest pedagogical strategies from all around with an ethos of tradition. In a world that teachers children that the survival of the fittest is the mantra of the day, we teach them to be ready to lend a helping hand, to be aware of their responsibilities as citizens of fragile world and ecosystem and to nurture nature. We help them to develop their mind, but to also give the same care and attention to their bodies by providing them with ample opportunities for different kinds of sport. We teach them to pay attention to the important issues of the day and to take a stand for themselves instead of blindly following the herd. We aim to establish a classroom environment where creativity and imagination can flourish. And, most important of all, besides textbook lessons, we teach our children important life lessons of mutual respect and cooperation.

We welcome you to our family and look forward to a long and mutually rewarding relationship.

Delhi World Public School, NARAPALLY

  • Operational Effectiveness for establishing high standards of performance
